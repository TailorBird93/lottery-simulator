How likely are you to win?

To calculate the odds of winning a lottery, you need to know the total number of possible combinations and the number of winning combinations.

Calculating lottery odds:

n! / (r! * (n-r)!)
Where:
n = total number of choices
r = number of selections made


So, using classic Lotto (choosing 6 numbers between 1-59):
59! / (6! * (59-6)!) = 45,057,474
Your chances of winning the lottery are 1:45,057,474
Keep that number in mind when you want to buy a ticket.
